boolean canProcess(byte[] segmentBytes,

Gets a value indicating whether the supplied byte data can be processed by this reader. This is not a guarantee that no errors will occur, but rather a best-effort indication of whether the parse is likely to succeed. Implementations are expected to check things such as the opening bytes, data length, etc.
